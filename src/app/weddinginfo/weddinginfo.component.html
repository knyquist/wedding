Wedding Information

  • Where
    The wedding ceremony & reception will take place at The Santa Cruz Museum of Art & History (MAH) located at 705 Front Street. The venue is located in the heart of downtown Santa Cruz.


  • When
    The wedding is on September 22, 2018. Please plan to arrive between 4 & 4:30 PM. The ceremony will begin at 5 PM followed by a cocktail hour, dinner, and dancing late into the night.

  • Weather
    We picked September in Santa Cruz for the weather! Expect sunny and mild weather with highs approaching 80 during the day and lows approaching the mid 50s during the night. The coast can be foggy, for sure, so bring layers!
